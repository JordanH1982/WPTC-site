Who We Are

Jordan H // @grislyaddams

Jordan is a musician, amatuer programmer and lover of all things plastic representations of his childhood. He has collected toys and doodled in the margins ever since he can remember and finally thought to parlay that into a business. Jordan loves all the classic 80's movies and cartoons, as well as kung-fu movies from the '70s on. When he's not being a total nerd, he plays drums in KCMO rock outfit AM Power Nap, as well as occasionally jamming with the other WPTC dudes. Jordan is founder of WPTC and owns way too much Nintendo paraphenalia.

Caleb W // @littledrumrboy86

Caleb is nothing more than a giant kid who loves movies, toys and music. He grew up loving all the great things the 80's and 90's had to offer. From Horror movies like Fright Night and Child's Play to toy lines like TMNT, Caleb loves it all! "To this day, I still hold strongly onto all those amazing memories through collecting the things that bring back the nostalgia!" Caleb is co-founder of WPTC and one of our talented graphic artists.

Mike M // @mikemudrytattoo

Mike is our resident Transformers and Sci-fi fan. Mike Co-owns Light Sleeper Tattoo in Lee's Summit, MO and is a hyper talented tattooist and artist. Mike also loves music and has played guitar in several touring bands! Mike most recently joined WPTC as a gifted graphic artist.
